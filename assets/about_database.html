This open-access database offers a curated collection of plant-derived natural compounds from agriculturally important Indian plants, designed to facilitate the identification of safe and effective therapeutic targets for various diseases.

Engineered for virtual screening, it allows researchers to discover novel natural compounds and provides structural data in optimized ".pdb" format.

The database offers detailed profiles encompassing physicochemical, pharmacokinetic, pharmacodynamic, and toxicological parameters, rendering it an invaluable resource for in-silico drug discovery and development process.
